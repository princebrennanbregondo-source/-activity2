Bregondo's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the warm milk with the yeast, granulated sugar, egg, and egg yolk.
  2. Melt the butter and add it to the mixture.
  3. Add the bread flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours until it doubles in size.
  6. Once risen, roll the dough out into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the dark brown sugar and ground cinnamon.
  8. Spread the cinnamon sugar filling evenly over the dough.
  9. Roll the dough tightly into a swiss roll shape.
  10. Cut the roll into 3cm thick sections and place them flat onto a baking tray.
